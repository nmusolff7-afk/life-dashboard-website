About the product

Built for people who already take their life seriously.

Life Dashboard exists for ambitious generalists who track across multiple systems and are tired of doing the stitching themselves.

Company identity

  • Product name: Life Dashboard

  • Primary contact: admin@lifedashboardapp.com

What the product is trying to solve

Most users already have a tracker for meals, another for workouts, another for money, and separate tools for email and schedule. The friction is not a lack of data. It is fragmentation.

Design philosophy

The product is designed to reduce cognitive load without reducing agency. It should feel calm, precise, and clearly helpful instead of preachy or performative.

How it is differentiated

The differentiator is not “AI” in the abstract. It is the combination of unified context, four-domain structure, deterministic scoring, and AI used intentionally where synthesis genuinely helps.
