Security and control

Designed for sensitive personal data without pretending to be something it is not.

Life Dashboard handles health, financial, calendar, and communication context. That requires a serious product posture around consent, visibility, and data handling.

User-controlled connections

  • Connections are optional.
  • Users can disconnect sources without giving up the entire product.
  • Manual logging remains available where appropriate.

AI is not the scoring engine

  • Core category scoring is deterministic rather than model-generated.
  • AI is used intentionally for synthesis, parsing, and user-invoked assistance.
  • The product is designed so not every dashboard action requires an AI call.

Read-only financial posture

  • Financial aggregation is intended to be read-only where supported by the connection provider.
  • The product is not built to move money, trade assets, or act as a financial institution.
  • Finance context exists to improve visibility and decision quality.

Clear deletion and disconnect paths

  • Users can request deletion of their account and data.
  • Connections can be revoked or disconnected.
  • Privacy questions can be directed to the business contact listed on this site.

What this page is for

This page exists to make the product’s data-control posture legible to users, reviewers, and partners. It is a product-facing summary, not a certification or compliance claim.

What it does not claim

Life Dashboard does not claim HIPAA status, medical-device status, or financial-advisor status. It does not present itself as a bank, broker, or healthcare provider.
