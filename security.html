Security and control

Designed for sensitive personal data with a narrow, reviewable product posture.

Life Dashboard handles health, financial, calendar, and communication context. That requires a serious approach to consent, access control, encryption, deletion, and reviewer clarity.

Governance and security contact

  • Life Dashboard is operated by Apex Leadership LLC.
  • Security and privacy oversight currently sits with Nathan Musolf, Founder.
  • Security inquiries can be sent to admin@lifedashboardapp.com.

Documented policies and access governance

  • Operational access to production systems is intended to be limited to authorized personnel only.
  • Administrative access is expected to follow least-privilege principles.
  • Security, privacy, access, and retention requirements are treated as product-operating requirements, not marketing claims.

User-controlled connections

  • Connections are optional.
  • Users can disconnect sources without giving up the entire product.
  • Manual logging remains available where appropriate.

MFA and account protection

  • Administrative access to critical systems should be protected with multi-factor authentication wherever supported.
  • Consumer-facing connection flows may also involve additional verification requirements presented by the underlying provider.
  • Authentication and access controls are intended to reduce unauthorized access to connected personal data.

Read-only financial posture

  • Financial aggregation is intended to be read-only where supported by the connection provider.
  • The product is not built to move money, initiate payments, lend money, trade assets, or act as a financial institution.
  • Finance context exists for personal finance tracking, budgeting visibility, and decision quality.

Encryption and hosted infrastructure

  • Data is intended to be protected in transit using modern TLS-based encryption.
  • Stored consumer data is intended to reside on infrastructure that supports encryption and operational security controls appropriate for hosted software products.
  • Connected-source data handling is designed around minimizing unnecessary exposure and limiting processing to product functionality.

Vulnerability management

  • Security maintenance includes dependency review, patching, and software-lifecycle attention as part of normal product operations.
  • Production systems and developer environments are expected to be maintained with current security updates and access protections.
  • Identified issues are intended to be reviewed and remediated according to risk and operational impact.

Deletion, retention, and disconnect paths

  • Users can request deletion of their account and data.
  • Connections can be revoked or disconnected.
  • Retention decisions are intended to balance product operations, legal obligations, fraud prevention, and user requests.

AI is not the scoring engine

  • Core category scoring is deterministic rather than model-generated.
  • AI is used intentionally for limited parsing and user-invoked assistance.
  • Connected fitness and activity data are not used for AI or machine-learning model training.
  • The product is designed so not every dashboard action requires an AI call.

What this page is for

This page exists to make the product's security and data-control posture legible to users, reviewers, and partners. It is a product-facing summary, not a certification or compliance claim.

What it does not claim

Life Dashboard does not claim HIPAA status, medical-device status, financial-advisor status, or bank/broker status. It is not presented as a payments product or financial institution.
